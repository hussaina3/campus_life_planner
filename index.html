About

What is this?

Campus Life Planner is a personal task and event tracker for students. Log study sessions, assignments, exams, and campus events — each with a duration, due date, and tag. It lives entirely in your browser and needs no account or server.

Features

  • Add, edit, and delete tasks with duration tracking
  • Regex-powered live search with match highlighting
  • Sort by date, title, or duration
  • Dashboard with 7-day activity trend chart
  • Daily duration cap with live notifications
  • Minutes ↔ hours unit conversion
  • Import and export your data as JSON
  • Fully keyboard navigable and screen reader friendly
